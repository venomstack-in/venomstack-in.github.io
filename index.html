Who We Are

About Venom Stack

We are a student-driven tech community dedicated to fostering innovation, learning, and collaboration among BCA students.

Our Mission

Venom Stack was founded with the belief that technology is best learned through doing. We host workshops, hackathons, coding contests, and tech talks to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world skills. Our community thrives on peer learning, mentorship, and the drive to build something meaningful.

Our Vision

To become the most active and impactful student tech community, producing industry-ready professionals who solve real problems through code.

Our Values

Collaboration over competition. Open source mindset. Continuous learning. Inclusive community where every member's contribution counts.
